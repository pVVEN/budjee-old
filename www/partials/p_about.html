About Budjee

Budjee is a basic self-budgeting app. It's designed to be very simple, and to provide immediate feedback on the state of your day-to-day finances. Its accuracy is dependent on the information provided, so it's best used as a general rough idea of what you have available. It's not meant to replace your banking app, just to provide a faster, slightly more anonymous glance at your funds.

This app was built using the following technologies:

  • jQuery
  • Bootstrap
  • AngularJS
    • angular-route
    • angular-touch
    • ngStorage
    • ui-bootstrap
  • Cordova
  • Eclipse and Android SDK
  • NodeJS
  • Git
  • Sublime Text
  • SimpLESS
